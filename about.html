About Me

Alessandro Samuel Tamada

I am a passionate Quality Assurance Engineer with over two years of experience in testing and ensuring the quality of software applications. My career began as a manual tester, where I developed a keen eye for detail and a strong ability to identify software defects and improve testing processes. I have experience working in dynamic industries like logistics and online driver apps, where I have been responsible for creating and executing detailed test cases for complex, end-to-end workflows.


I specialize in API and backend testing, utilizing tools such as Postman, JIRA, TestRail, MongoDB, and Xray. I am currently expanding my skill set by learning automation testing tools, including Selenium with Python and Java, as I transition towards a more automated approach to QA. My commitment to continuous learning drives me to seek out innovative solutions to testing challenges. I look forward to contributing to teams that prioritize quality, efficiency, and a seamless user experience.
